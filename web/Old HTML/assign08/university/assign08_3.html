Henchman and Bodyguard Training Division

This is a brand new Division of EU. Even the greatest magicians need help sometimes. Sure, magic is great, but oftentimes it still doesn't compare with good, old-fashioned teamwork. After lengthy deliberation, Ether University has decided to begin this program that will prepare the best assistants for our students and for the magical community at large.

-- EU Administration
